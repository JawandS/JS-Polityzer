Tackling the issues that affect you.

Tackling the issues that affect you.

Joshua has a proven track record delivering results.

As a resident since childhood, Joshua has not only seen the troubles that Fredericksburg, Stafford, and Spotsylvania face, he's made strides to fix them as a public servant and delegate. As a son of the region, the welfare of its people his primary mission as a leader.

His work proceeds him, and Joshua has championed a government that serves the people. Whether cutting through red tape or reaching across the aisle of government, Joshua delivered common-sense legislation to help all Virginians.

  • Solutions for traffic and I-95 congestion
  • Protecting reproductive freedom
  • Housing for all Virginians
  • Supporting public education and our educators
  • An economy for all
