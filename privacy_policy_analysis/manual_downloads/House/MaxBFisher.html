WE CAN DO BETTER TOGETHER

SecureYourVoteVirginia.com

 

Politics is undermining our potential to get things done. Politics of division has no place in Virginia! I am a uniter, not a divider, and we can do better TOGETHER. Your opinion is what matters, support common ground solutions, and unite as we make up the spirit of Virginia!

 

Commonsense solutions are coming to northern Virginia! I have spent most of my adult life in the greater Herndon area! It is easy to get drawn into the drama of the national media, but it is important to realize we can't hide the problems we face in our homes daily. It is all about shared values that bring people together! This is our time as Virginians to stand up for the values that we hold dear. We are standing up for the future of our commonwealth and our community. We have been told too many times that more government control is the answer...

As your delegate, we can build a new day together. I will fight for you to protect the values that are important to YOU. I make a pledge to you to be open and seek input from YOU!

 

Keep our community safe by ensuring police and fire rescue have the training and resources they need. We need to publicly support our first responders and ensure they have the tools they need to enforce the laws. 

 

Bolster our communities' workforces by working closely with small, local business and community leaders on smart growth in the area. 

 

As your delegate I will fight for your religious freedoms and support legislation that protects the rights of Virginians to worship the faith of their choosing without government interference.

 

I’ll make sure right or left leaning political ideology isn’t inserted into classroom curriculum. Our students should receive an education free of political biases. We deserve to have high standards and parental involvement in education.
